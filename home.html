The Problem

Construction SMEs Face Critical, Unaddressed Financial Risks

The UK construction sector is the largest source of HMRC compliance failures. Existing tools are built for generic businesses — not the complexity of CIS deductions, reverse charge VAT, and multi-tier subcontracting.

CIS Misreporting Penalties

Manual CIS returns introduce systematic errors exposing firms to HMRC penalties of up to £3,000 per filing.

Subcontractor Payment Fraud

Ghost workers, duplicate invoices, and irregular patterns go undetected in spreadsheet-based systems used by 76% of SME contractors.

Irregular Cost Allocations

Project cost misallocation inflates margins and distorts cash flow forecasting without any early warning indicators.

VAT Reverse Charge Gaps

The 2021 domestic reverse charge for construction remains widely misapplied, creating declaration risks SMEs cannot manage manually.
